## 汽车上被洪水损坏的痕迹

那么，具体来说，你应该寻找什么呢？以下是一些最常见的迹象， [来自《消费者报告》](https://www.consumerreports.org/buying-a-car/beware-the-flood-of-flood-cars/) :

> 检查地毯看它们是否有被水浸泡过的迹象，比如有霉味或者粘有泥块。旧车上的全新地毯可能是另一个危险信号。*   **Check the seat mounting screw** to see if there is any sign of removal. In order to effectively dry the carpet, the seat must be removed, and maybe even replaced.*   **Check the light.** A visible waterline may still appear on the lens or reflector.*   **Check the places that are difficult to clean,** such as the gap between the trunk and the lower panel of the hood for dirt and debris.*   **Look at the bottom edge** of the bracket or panel, where dirt is usually not deposited.*   **Check the head of any unpainted and exposed screw** under the instrument panel. Flooded cars, exposed metal will show signs of rust.*   **Check whether the rubber drain plugs** at the bottom of the vehicle and the door look like they have been removed recently.
