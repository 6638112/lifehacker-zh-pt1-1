### 如何展示美国国旗

这不仅仅是把旗子挂在窗户上，然后结束一天的事情。以下是显示它的规则:

*   **在同一根旗杆上:**美国国旗在最高点，高于任何其他国旗。
*   **分组:**美国国旗自行其是。其他国家的国旗也在同样的高度飘扬。
*   **在扬声器平台上:**用扬声器平台显示时，必须在扬声器的上方和后方。如果安装在五线谱上，它位于扬声器的右侧。
*   **装饰:**千万不要用国旗做装饰。使用彩旗，蓝色在上面，然后白色，然后红色。
*   在特殊的日子，可以降半旗。在阵亡将士纪念日，旗帜降半旗直到中午，然后升起。

#### 展示和传递旗帜的其他规则

> *   Don't let the flag touch the ground.
> *   Do not hang the flag upside down unless there is an emergency.
> *   Do not lay the flag flat, or carry articles in the flag.
> *   Don't make clothes with the national flag. Don't store the flag in a dirty place.
> *   Don't use it as a cover.
> *   Don't tie it or tie it back. Always let it fall freely.
> *   Don't draw pictures or make other marks on the national flag.

### 如何正确折叠美国国旗

VFW 网站 的 [有图片，如果你是一个视觉学习者，但是它们的说明如下:](https://www.vfw.org/community/flag-etiquette)

> 1.  Fold the striped part under the flag on the blue area.
> 2.  The folded edge is then folded in half to meet the open edge.
> 3.  Then start the triangle folding by bringing the stripe angle of the folded edge to the open edge.
> 4.  The outer point then rotates inward parallel to the open edge, forming a second triangle.
> 5.  Folding the triangle continues until the entire length of the flag is folded into a triangle, and only the blue area is visible.
