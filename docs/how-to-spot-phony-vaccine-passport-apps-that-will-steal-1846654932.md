# 如何识别会窃取你数据的虚假“疫苗护照”应用程序

> 原文：<https://lifehacker.com/how-to-spot-phony-vaccine-passport-apps-that-will-steal-1846654932>

作为一个政策问题，疫苗护照的使用仍然不清楚。在美国没有官方的、联邦政府认可的“疫苗护照”，但是 T2 的公司已经在为那些想要旅行或参加大型活动的接种过疫苗的人开发护照应用程序。随后，疫苗护照的模糊状态为骗子窃取你的个人信息提供了机会，这就是为什么 [商业改善局发布了关于虚假应用的警告](https://www.bbb.org/article/scams/24048-bbb-scam-alert-covid-19-vaccine-passports-are-coming-scams-are-sure-to-follow) 。

### **注意什么**

有一些合法的应用程序已经在使用了。纽约州推出了 [Excelsior Pass](https://covid19vaccine.health.ny.gov/excelsior-pass) ，它使用该州的疫苗登记处来确认疫苗接种状态，像 [美国航空公司和捷蓝航空公司也使用应用程序](https://www.npr.org/2021/04/08/985400141/faq-what-is-a-vaccine-passport-and-what-are-these-credentials-used-for) 来确认航班的阴性 COVID 测试。

然而，由于白宫拒绝使用疫苗护照，我们可能不会看到一个单一的，集中的应用程序将被所有美国人使用。相反，我们可能会看到越来越多由私营公司开发的应用程序，供地方或州一级使用。但这也将使识别骗子运行的虚假应用程序变得更加困难，这就是为什么你应该格外小心你的个人信息，并验证该应用程序是否合法。

考虑到这一点，商业改善局有以下建议:

> *   **Be skeptical of any vaccine passport app that claims to be from the U.S. federal government.** [At present, the U.S. federal government has no plans to create a national vaccine passport](https://thehill.com/policy/healthcare/546467-fauci-says-federal-government-wont-mandate-vaccine-passports?rl=1) . E-mails, phone calls and text messages claiming that the government needs this passport are probably frauds.
> *   **Flying or participating in activities? Check with the company directly.** You may need to show proof of COVID-19 negative test or vaccine to participate in the event or board the plane. As with all things related to COVID-19, policies change frequently. Be sure to check with your airline, sports team, activity venue, etc. To get the latest details.
> *   **Study** carefully. If you receive an invitation to download the COVID-19 Vaccine Passport application, please make sure to do research before entering your personal information. Liars are very creative, so be suspicious of anything that seems too good or too crazy to be true. Check all information carefully against official news sources and company websites.
> *   **Guard your official number** . Never give your Medicare ID number, social security number, health plan information or bank information to anyone you don't know or trust. [Don't post your vaccine card on social media](https://www.bbb.org/article/news-releases/23675-bbb-tip-dont-share-your-vaccine-card-on-social-media) .
> *   **Think the link may be real? Please check the URL carefully. Scammers often buy domain names that look like official websites for fraud. Please take care to ensure that the destination of the link is really what it claims to be. If the message claims to be from the government, make sure the URL ends with. Gov (USA) or. Ca (for Canada). In case of doubt, please conduct a separate internet search on this website or call its source directly.**
