# 如何洗涤节日丝袜和其他织物装饰品

> 原文：<https://lifehacker.com/how-to-wash-holiday-stockings-and-other-fabric-decorati-1848124966>

也许你是这样一种人，在每个假期结束时，你会把所有的袜子和其他节日织物装饰品洗干净，然后放进一个精心密封的容器里，这样第二年一切都准备好了。

或者可能到了一月的第一周(或者每当你取下装饰品的时候)，你已经筋疲力尽，厌倦了看这些东西，于是你把袜子、树裙和枕头扔进了一个盒子，扔进了阁楼。

如果你属于第二类，你现在可能会拿起那些节日装饰品盒子，并注意到它们有点脏或看起来很脏。但不用担心——大部分(如果不是全部)都可以洗。在 [一篇关于云杉](https://www.thespruce.com/clean-christmas-home-accessories-2146906) 的文章中，玛丽·马洛·莱弗里特向我们介绍了如何做到这一点。以下是需要了解的内容。

## 节日丝袜怎么洗

丝袜有多种材质，所以 [莱弗里特按照面料](https://www.thespruce.com/clean-christmas-home-accessories-2146906) 来分解:

> *   **Velvet:** Silk stockings made of velvet should be dry-cleaned by professional cleaners. If they are wrinkled, never iron them with an iron, or you will leave a permanent mark on the soleplate of the iron. Lift the fibers with steam and make them silky smooth with a soft brush.
> *   **Embroidery:** Embroidered stockings are beautiful, and it took someone a long time to create them. To brighten the color, give it a good cleaning with an interior brush. If they are stained, take them to a professional dry cleaner.
> *   **Cross-stitch:** Cross-stitch is generally done on fabrics that can be washed by hand. Be sure to test the color fastness of the thread before continuing.
> *   **Felt:** Felt stockings should also go to the dry cleaners. Felt shrinks and fades easily and should not be soaked in water. If the socks are decorated with sequins or beads, and if the decorations are glued on, any kind of cleaning may destroy the design. Be careful. To remove dust, put socks in a mesh bag, then put them in the dryer and turn on the unheated air circulation.
> *   **Knitting or crocheting:** These stockings are the easiest to clean. Hand washing with mild detergent in warm water is like washing a sweater. Rinse well-don't wring out the water, or the fabric will stretch. Flatten and reshape into the correct size.
> *   **Wool:** Wool-woven stockings can be washed by hand with warm water or sent to a professional laundry. If necessary, dry it and flatten it with a press cloth.
> *   **Quilting:** Quilted stockings should be washed by hand with cold water to prevent fading or bleeding. Rinse and dry.

## 圣诞树裙

最理想的情况是，你想要立即处理圣诞树裙上的任何污渍——最常见的是污垢/灰尘、树液、动物尿液和水渍，——莱弗里特说。但是如果没有发生，用一把钝的菜刀轻轻刮掉任何固体/硬化的污渍(如蜡烛蜡)。之后，用喷雾瓶里的冷水浸透污渍，然后用干净的白布吸干，她解释道。尽管这很诱人，但不要擦洗污渍(这会损害布料)。

一旦完成，如果织物是可洗的，继续按照它的说明洗。如果它被珠子、贴花、亮片或类似的东西覆盖，莱弗里特建议把它送到干洗店。

## 节日披巾、毯子和枕头

对于大多数衣物，你可以简单地按照标签上的清洁说明去做——无论是手洗、放入洗衣机还是干洗。检查抱枕，看看它们是否有可拆卸的外套，如果有，按照它们的说明操作。

如果你有刺绣或装饰过的装饰枕头，并且没有可拆卸的枕套， [莱弗里特建议](https://www.thespruce.com/clean-christmas-home-accessories-2146906) 把它们放在网袋里在凉爽的环境下烘干。
